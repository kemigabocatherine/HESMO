HESMO CHRISTIAN RELIEF

Our Story

Pastor Edward Arnold Kayemba starts HESMO CHRISTIAN RELIEF after three years of paying school fees for 24 underprivileged children and taking care of 15 single mothers suffering under the yoke of poverty. The inception of HESMO was fuelled by a phone call between Mr. Kayemba and a church friend who detailed to him the ordeal of a single mother to a newly born who was forced to leave hospital and spend a night in the cold of a church veranda with her infant child because she did not have money to cover her hospital bills. Obeying to the voice of God, Mr. Kayemba started this ministry that would offer both spiritual and physical deliverance to single mothers and less privileged children by biblical teaching of the gospel of deliverance and a steady supply of basic needs.

About Us

We are a Christian humanitarian relief and a Church based in Uganda devoted to transforming lives of single mothers and less privileged children through teaching the gospel of deliverance and a steady supply of basic needs with an aim of ending poverty and illiteracy.
